Check out some of the things that Team Alpha offers!

Guaranteed Rides

Fancy a sedan, van, or a limo? Just a few taps and your ride will come to you! If there are no drivers nearby, our administrators will make sure to send an awesome driver from few kilometres away, in no time!

Accurate Pay

Your fare will be shown to you before you actually request a ride. Once you reach your destination, you will pay for the exact same amount. No additional costs, no tips, nothing to hassle you!

Proven and Trusted

Our drivers were thoroughly screened before we allow them to participate in our network. While on your ride, they are guaranteed to treat you with utmost respect and courtesy. If you feel otherwise, you can always tap that panic button in our app.

Pay By Cash Or Card

You can pay your driver by cash when you reach your destination, or go cash-less by registering your credit card and pay your fare with it!
